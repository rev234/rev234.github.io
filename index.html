Wow! What a ride. Scentsy is amazing.
I started Scentsy in October 2007 after seeing it at a home show. I thought it would be great for Christmas gifts. I could buy it for my gifts, get the discount and be done with it.
I took my testers into work and sheepishly put them out on a small side table. I didn't tell anyone they were there or that they were mine. I came back after break to find about $150 in orders. Next break, another $150 in orders. I just couldn't believe how much everyone fell in love with this product, just like I did.
In my first 70 days I sold over $5300 in Scentsy product without having to do much at all and I was on my way to a new LOVE! I am currently a director and have enjoyed many trips with Scentsy and LOVE teaching my team how to reach their goals.
